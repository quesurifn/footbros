The Tapcentive Touchpoint - a visible call to action to trigger mobile engagement with a tap.

Touchpoint Summary


    With the Touchpoint, you can:
  • Train consumers to engage over and over again
  • Drive foot traffic where and when you want it
  • Reach both iOS and Android users
  • Receive daily diagnostic updates
  • Customize the look and feel

Bluetooth Beacon
Bluetooth Smart Ready provides device reach across iOS 7.1+ and Android 4.3+.

Near Field Communication
NFC Type 4 chip provides reach to Android users with or without your mobile app.

Cellular Radio
2.5/3G GSM module for remote management via the Tapcentive Manager.
